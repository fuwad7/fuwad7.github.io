Quality Room

A haven of tranquility in the midst of an exciting city, the spacious hotel rooms and suites at The Mermaid Pearl, Cox's Bazar offer exquisite furnishings, opulent marble bathrooms and premium appointments. Embrace the timeless luxury that awaits you.

Private Beach

Glorious views of sea are provided at all of our five star hotels and luxury villas affording you the opportunity to explore the crystal clear waters of the Adriatic. Guests will enjoy perfectly comfortable rooms, exquisite luxury interiors and captivating service from our concierges.

Best Accommodation

Our suites of contemporary conference centres are both impressive and flexible. Plush interior design, swathes of natural sunlight and picture perfect panoramas of the sea make them just as popular a choice for weddings and other tailor-made events as for business.

Wellness & Spa

The spa at The Mermaid Pearl, Cox's Bazar is the most sophisticated spa in the area and receives its practice through the delivery of superb massage and facial treatments. As the spa pays tribute to the culture and rich heritage of the local area, the spa provides a chance for total relaxation & recovery using natural ingredients.
